Our Comprehensive BPO Financial Services

Record to Report (R2R)

Comprehensive management of financial data from collection to reporting, ensuring accurate financial statements and strategic insights. Includes data gathering, journal entries, account reconciliation, financial close, consolidation, and regulatory submissions.

Order to Cash (O2C)

End-to-end management of the sales cycle, from order placement to cash collection. Includes order management, credit management, order fulfillment, invoicing, accounts receivable, cash application, and collections.

Procure to Pay (P2P)

Managing the entire procurement lifecycle, from identifying needs to final supplier payment. Covers requisitioning, sourcing, purchase order creation, receiving, invoice processing, and payment execution.
